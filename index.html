What is CoSolve

A methodology.
A platform.
One outcome: solutions.

Most organisations are skilled at identifying problems. What they lack is a structured, repeatable system for solving them — collaboratively, at the right level, with the right people.

CoSolve is that system. Built for senior leaders, it combines a battle-tested problem-solving methodology with a cloud platform where AI agents work alongside your team — structuring inputs, surfacing patterns, and keeping the process on track — from problem definition through to aligned, committed action.

How to Engage

CoSolve works
the way your organisation works

There is no one-size-fits-all approach to organisational problem-solving. CoSolve adapts to your context — whether you need a focused sprint, a continuous capability, or a structured transformation journey.

Engagement Model 01
Intensive Problem-Solving Session
A focused, time-bounded engagement — typically one to three days — where CoSolve facilitates a senior leadership team through a specific, high-priority organisational challenge from framing through to actionable resolution.
Ideal for: Urgent strategic decisions, board-level alignment challenges, post-merger integration issues, crisis response planning.

Outcome: A fully documented solution plan with assigned ownership and immediate next steps — leaving the session ready to execute.
Engagement Model 02
Ongoing Problem-Solving Capability
A subscription-based model where CoSolve becomes an embedded platform within the organisation — enabling leadership teams to initiate, run, and track structured problem-solving cycles on demand, across departments and business units.
Ideal for: Companies committing to continuous improvement, organisations managing multiple concurrent strategic initiatives, leadership teams that want structured decision-making as a core competency.

Outcome: A sustainable internal capability that reduces dependence on external consultants and accelerates the pace of change.
Engagement Model 03
Company-Wide Problem-Solving Strategy
A transformational deployment where ValueSims partners with your organisation to build a company-wide problem-solving culture — embedding CoSolve's methodology across all levels, from the C-suite to frontline teams.
Ideal for: Organisations undergoing large-scale transformation, companies where problem-solving capability is fragmented or inconsistent, CEOs building a high-performance leadership culture.

Outcome: A unified methodology, shared language, and measurable organisational capability — tracked through the CoSolve platform.

About ValueSims

The company behind CoSolve

ValueSims is an Austrian technology and advisory company specialising in the design and delivery of structured problem-solving solutions for senior leadership teams. Founded and led by Vassilis Pangalis, ValueSims combines deep expertise in business methodology, digital simulation, and artificial intelligence to build tools that help organisations make better decisions — faster and with greater confidence.

CoSolve is ValueSims' flagship platform — the result of years of working with organisations across multiple industries to understand why structured problem-solving succeeds when traditional approaches fail.
